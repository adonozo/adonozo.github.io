Lenguas


An Android application to learn native languages

Created as a native Android application, it is compatible with older devices running Android 4.1 and up. The app has a very small size - just above 4 MB!

This project is the result of a joint effort by the Iberoamerican Organization Statesand its multidisciplinary teams that include linguists, graphic designers, sound engineers, and developers. I personally contributed with the design and development of the Android application, and with the maintenance of the underlying service that makes the app work.


Native Android Development

Some benefits of creating a native Android app are the small footprint, efficient memory usage, and access to peripherals. The app is available in the Play Store, but the server is no longer maintained so it may have a very limited functionality.

The app follows the Activity Lifecycle and the Fragment Lifecycle. Besides, it uses libraries from the Android ecosystem which work nicely together. These includes:

  • Retrofit
  • RxJava
  • Gson
  • Room
  • Dagger
